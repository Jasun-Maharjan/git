About Norbu


For the luxury and the best simplicity

Norbu is a watch e-commerce website that offers luxurious and grand watches to its customer. We provide great customer satisfaction and easy purchases.



Norbu, which means gems in tibetan language, tries the best to offer their customers and investors the best quality and comfort in terms of investing, options and shopping. It thrives to offer beautiful watches to their customers in return for their satisfaction. It wants their customers to shop in their website with a smile on their faces and always wants to meet up with their expectations. Their customers drive them further to develop their website and offer more to their customers.
