The impact of technology on the watch industry and consumer trends.

We all keep on loosing track of time. Yet, somehow we get back on track. All thanks to new technologies. As time passes by, so does trends. But time has always been trending, is and forever will. Technology has changed the course of the landscape of the watch industry. Consumer preferences are also evolving. Promoting new developments, influencing consumer behaviour, and creating new opportunities for both emerging and established brands.Digital marketing strategies, industry social media campaigns and influencer collaborations have become crucial for building brand awareness and connecting with technology aware buyers. Moreover ,the focus is shifting towards interactive consumers experiences seeking beyond simply owning a watch. Smart watches have changed the landscape of the market, offering a compelling blend of timekeeping and advanced features. From fitness tracking to instant communication ,these devices cater to a modern approach, while some embrace the duality and some view them as a threat to traditional watches. New movements and materials push the boundaries of horological engineering, resulting in watches that are not only functional but also artistic expressions of ingenuity.
